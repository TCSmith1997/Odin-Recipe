Hot Cheeto Burrito Recipe

Description

The Hot Cheeto Burrito combines all of the most beloved aspects of a great burrito. Theres the spiciness from the hot cheetos and chile con queso that gets alleviated by the soothing notes that come from the lime soaked guacamole. Any choice of protien will go fantastically with this dish.
  1. Cook meat of your choice (Beef, Chicken, Lamb, etc.)
  2. Mash 1 avacado per person
  3. Heat Tortilla in microwave for 5-10 seconds depending on size
  4. Spread guac on toritlla
  5. Put one scoop of rice on toritlla
  6. Place one scoop of meat on tortilla
  7. Drizzle chile con queso on top
  8. Sprinkle as many hot cheetos on top as youd like
