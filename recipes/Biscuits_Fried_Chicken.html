Fried Chicken and Buscuits

Description

If you love crispy fried chicken with a soft fluffy and buttery biscuit, you have found the right recipe. This dish will have you wondering why the popeyes chicken sandwhich even went viral. Before we get started, remember that the temperature to cook chicken to is always 165 degrees farenheit.

Ingredients

  • Chicken(Breast, or Legs)
  • Peanut Oil
  • Spices Blend
  • Biscuits
  • Topping of Your Choice (Jam, Honey, etc.)

Recipe Steps

  1. Heat three quarts of peanut oil to 340-360 degrees farenheit
  2. Place Chicken in for about 12-14 minutes and check the temperature
  3. Remove Chicken and place on top of biscuit
  4. Drizzle with honey and serve!
